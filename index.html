About SomaStory

A Platform To Connect Dermatologists With Patients To Achieve Better Skin Treatment

Patients are able to track their daily progress, share reports, and chat directly with their providers.

- Complete a daily check-in to track progress and monitor side effects.
- Share reports with your provider without visiting the office.
- Ask questions and alert your provider of any changes with the chat feature.
Providers are able to update treatment plans, view patient reports, and send messages to their patients.

- Adjust treatment plans instantly through Soma Story so patients do not have to wait until the next visit for updates.
- Check up on your patients progress by puling up their weekly or monthly report.
- Message patients directly and stay in constant communication thus increasing recovery rates.
